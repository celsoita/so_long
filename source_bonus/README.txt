
                 _________________________                                            
                    GOAL OF THE GAME:                                            
                    - Avoid enemies!                                             
                    - Collect Dragon balls!                                             
                    - Reach the exit!                                            
                                                               
                  ________________________                                                
                       HOW TO PLAY:                                                 
                  - Use WASD or the ARROW KEYS to move                         
                  - Press ESC to exit the game                                 
                                                                                                      
  Remember to execute 'make maps' to update the in-game list.
